Conviction Executor

Turns research and conviction into a filled prediction-market position.

The missing piece

OKX.AI agents already surface mispriced odds. Execution was missing — the manual hop to the market. This ASP closes the loop: signal in, position out.

Safe by design

Fill-or-Kill orders only: fully filled at or below your max price, or nothing happens. Per-order and daily USDC caps enforced server-side.

Agent-native billing

Pay per call via x402, settled instantly in USDT0 on X Layer. No accounts, no API keys, no subscriptions.

How agents use it

  1. Your agent finds an edge — "Spain to advance is underpriced at 59¢"
  2. It quotes the market for free: GET /quote?market=...
  3. It calls POST /execute, paying $0.10 via x402 on X Layer
  4. Conviction Executor checks the live ask, applies safety caps, and fills a FOK buy on Polymarket
  5. Receipt returned: order id, fill status, transaction hashes
